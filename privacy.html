HungryPaw Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Data We Collect

HungryPaw stores your pet feeding data — pet names, meal logs, weight entries, and feeding schedules — to provide the service. All data is stored securely in your personal Supabase database and on your device.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display feeding history and insights within the app
  • To sync data across your devices via your account
  • To send optional feeding reminder notifications

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. Your pet feeding data stays in your account and on your device. HungerPaw has no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no tracking.

Data Retention

Your data remains in your account until you delete it or delete your account. Deleting the app does not delete your cloud data — you must sign out or delete your account to remove server data.

Your Rights

You can export all your data at any time (Settings → Export Data). You can delete your data by removing pets and meals in the app, or by contacting us at the email below for full account deletion.
